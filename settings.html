Settings & Guide

Voice Language

Best Result Tips 💡

  • Speak one item naturally: "চিনি ৩ কেজি প্রতি কেজি ৩০ টাকা".
  • Pause slightly between items so the app can split them better.
  • Say quantity and price clearly for clothes, cosmetics, or mixed bills.

User Guide

  • Go to "Current Bill", tap the big green Mic button and dictate the bill.
  • The table updates live while you speak.
  • To remove a mistaken row, click the red '×' button on the row.
  • Once finished, press the Print button (Requires Bluetooth Printer app integration).
